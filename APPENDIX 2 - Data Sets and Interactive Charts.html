Hourly Data

There are 6 key temperatures in the observed system, where the temperature Thot becomes the temperature Tdelivered. With this in mind, the number of pairs for determining temperature differences was determined by the method of combinations without repetition according to the principle shown below. In this particular case, there are five elements of the second class, apropos:

NOMENCLATURE

A = Δ|Tambient - Tcold|

B = Δ|Tambient - Tdelivered|

C = Δ|Tambient - Tmains|

D = Δ|Tambient - Ttank|

E = Δ|Tcold - Tdelivered|

F = Δ|Tcold - Tmains|

G = Δ|Tcold - Ttank|

I = Δ|Tdelivered - Tmains|

J = Δ|Tdelivered - Ttank|

H = Δ|Tmains - Ttank|
